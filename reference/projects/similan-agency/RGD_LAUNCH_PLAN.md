## Launch Overview

**Goal:** Establish thought leadership, increase B2B pipeline, improve online presence, position as premium local supplier

**Key messaging:**
- Local-first, family-owned (authenticity)
- 10+ years supplying 4/5-star hotels & restaurants (credibility)
- Quality > volume, sustainability matters (values)

**Target audience:**
- Primary: 4/5-star hotel F&B managers, executive chefs, restaurant owners (70%)
- Secondary: Western consumers (online retail, in-store) (20%)
- Tertiary: Commercial kitchen managers, event planners (10%)

**Budget:** 10,000 THB services + USD 100/month Meta ads + LinkedIn engagement (new)

**Key difference from F&B brands:** B2B tone (professional, credibility-focused) + LinkedIn as primary platform

## Paid Advertising Strategy

### Week 1 (Launch week): USD 50/week B2B + USD 50/week B2C

**B2B Campaign (LinkedIn + Facebook):**
- Audience: Hotel F&B managers, executive chefs, restaurant owners (Thailand, ages 30–60)
- Creative: Thought leadership articles, case studies, testimonial
- Budget: USD 50/week
- KPI: 500–800 impressions/week, 10–15 profile visits, 2–3 lead inquiries

**B2C Campaign (Instagram + Facebook):**
- Audience: Phuket-based, ages 25–55, interests in food, health, premium products
- Creative: Product carousel, lifestyle imagery, offer
- Budget: USD 50/week
- KPI: 800–1,200 impressions/week, 15–25 website visits, 2–4 online orders

### Week 2–4: Increase to USD 100/week (allocate based on performance)

- If B2B performing well: 70% B2B, 30% B2C (USD 70 / USD 30)
- If B2C performing well: 50% B2B, 50% B2C (even split)
- Track ROI per segment
