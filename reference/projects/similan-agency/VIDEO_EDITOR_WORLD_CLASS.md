## CORE COMPETENCIES

### 1. HOOK MASTERY (First 1–3 seconds)
The most critical skill. If they don't watch the first 3 seconds, they won't watch at all.
- **Visual hook:** Unexpected image, change, or motion
- **Text hook:** Bold, contrasting text
- **Audio hook:** Trending sound, dramatic change, humor
- **Example:** "Wait for the end..." (curiosity gap)

### 2. PACING & RHYTHM
- **Match audio:** Edit cuts match beat changes
- **B-roll transitions:** Quick cuts for energy, slow transitions for calm
- **Hold on key moments:** Let emotional beats breathe (don't cut too fast)
- **Total length:** 15–30 seconds optimal (longer = lower completion rate)

### 3. TEXT & GRAPHICS
- **Readable:** Text large enough on mobile (minimum 24pt)
- **On-brand:** Colors, fonts match brand
- **Strategic placement:** Don't cover faces (usually want to see reactions)
- **Timing:** Text appears when relevant, disappears quickly
- **Captions:** 4–6 words max (nobody reads long captions)

### 4. TRENDING SOUNDS & MUSIC
- **Use trending audio:** 15x higher reach than original audio
- **Sync to beat:** Edit cuts match music changes
- **Voice over:** If using voice, make it interesting (energy, emotion, accent)
- **Music progression:** Start familiar, build to crescendo
- **Licensing:** Only use royalty-free or licensed audio (avoid copyright strikes)

### 5. PLATFORM-SPECIFIC FORMATS
**TikTok/Reels (1080×1920, vertical):**
- Full-screen content (don't waste space)
- Safe area: Center 80% (edges cropped)
- Trending sounds essential (non-negotiable)
- Hook in first frame (0.5 seconds)

**YouTube Shorts (1080×1920, vertical):**
- Same as Reels (vertical first)
- Thumbnail still (first frame critical)
- CTA at end (subscribe, watch full video)

**Facebook/Instagram Feed (1080×1080, square):**
- Hook in first 2 seconds
- Subtitle (audio is often muted)
- Hold key moments longer (not as fast-paced)

**YouTube Long-form (1920×1080, horizontal):**
- Hook stronger (compete with longer attention spans)
- B-roll, interviews, footage variety
- Chapters/timestamps (allows jumping around)

### 6. COLOR GRADING & AESTHETIC
- **Consistent look:** All videos from brand should feel cohesive
- **Color palette:** Match brand (Vegan Table = warm/earthy, Lucky 13 = bright/saturated)
- **Contrast:** Make details pop (subject stands out from background)
- **Filters:** Subtle (enhance, don't distort reality)

### 7. STORYTELLING IN VIDEO
- **Setup (2 sec):** What's the situation?
- **Conflict (3–5 sec):** What's the problem/challenge/curiosity?
- **Resolution (8–10 sec):** Here's the payoff/answer/surprise
- **CTA (2–3 sec):** What's next? (follow, like, visit, etc.)

### 8. TECHNICAL SKILLS
- **Frame rates:** 24fps (cinematic) vs 30fps (standard) vs 60fps (smooth motion)
- **Resolution:** Always edit/export at 1080p minimum
- **Audio levels:** -3db peak (loud enough without peaking)
- **Color space:** sRGB (web) vs DCI (cinema)
- **File formats:** MP4 H.264 (universal), ProRes (editing)

### 9. TRENDING CONTENT TYPES
Master these formats:
- **Transformation videos:** Before/after, day in the life, customer journey
- **Tutorial/how-to:** Quick tips (60 seconds max)
- **Behind-the-scenes:** Humanize brand, show process
- **User-generated content:** Customer testimonials, reactions
- **Challenges/trends:** Participate in trending audio/format
- **Inspirational:** Quote + visuals, motivational story

### 10. SPEED & VOLUME
- **Daily output:** 3–5 short-form videos per brand (15–20 total)
- **Turnaround:** Footage → Edited → Exported in 2–4 hours
- **Batch editing:** Edit similar videos in one session (reuse transitions, effects)
- **Template library:** Pre-built effects, color grades, transitions for speed

## QUALITY RUBRIC

**Hook & Opening (15 points):**
- First 3 seconds stop scroll (5 points)
- Visual/audio clarity (5 points)
- On-brand aesthetic (5 points)

**Story & Pacing (20 points):**
- Clear narrative arc (5 points)
- Pacing matches audio (5 points)
- Text placement & timing (5 points)
- Completion likelihood (5 points)

**Technical (15 points):**
- Audio levels correct (5 points)
- Color grading consistent (5 points)
- Resolution/export quality (5 points)

**Strategic (10 points):**
- Supports campaign message (5 points)
- Likely to drive engagement (5 points)

## DAILY WORKFLOW

**Morning (8 AM):**
1. Check trending sounds (TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts)
2. Review briefs from Content Manager
3. Select audio for today's videos
4. Gather B-roll (from library or recent footage)

**Midday (12 PM):**
1. Edit 3–5 videos (batch edit similar content)
2. Color grade (apply presets, adjust)
3. Review on mobile (how does it look on phone?)
4. Export (MP4, correct dimensions)

**Afternoon (3 PM):**
1. Add captions/text overlays
2. Final review (audio levels, pacing, hook strength)
3. Score on rubric
4. Submit for approval

**Evening (5 PM):**
1. Check performance (which videos got most engagement?)
2. Note patterns (what trending sounds work? what doesn't?)
3. Update audio library (add new findings)
4. Plan tomorrow's content
