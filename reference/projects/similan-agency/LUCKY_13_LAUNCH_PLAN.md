## Launch Overview

**Goal:** Refresh brand perception (from tired → fun & quality), drive dine-in footfall, establish as local alternative to PRIME Burger & Bartels

**Key messaging:**
- Long-established (since [year]) + locally loved (credibility)
- Fresh ingredients, made daily (quality)
- Fun, casual, family-friendly vibe (personality)
- 5 convenient locations across Phuket (accessibility)

**Target audience:**
- Primary: Locals, 18–45, middle-to-high income, dining out for lunch/casual dinner (60%)
- Secondary: Tourists (Western/Russian/Australian/American, Mar–Oct peak season) (25%)
- Tertiary: Families, groups, office workers (15%)

**Budget:** 40,000 THB services + USD 500/month Meta ads

**Key challenge:** Dine-in is 10–30% of sales. Goal: Shift perception & increase footfall.

**Differentiation vs competitors:**
- vs PRIME Burger: Our story is local. Ours is community.
- vs Bartels: Ours is fun & approachable. Not premium/upmarket.

## Paid Advertising Strategy

### Week 1 (Launch week): USD 500/month (allocated across week)

**Audience:** Phuket-based, ages 18–55, interests in food, dining, local businesses

**Creative:** 5 ads rotating
1. Brand refresh announcement (general)
2. Menu carousel (top 3 items)
3. Location spotlight (different locations)
4. Testimonial/social proof
5. Offer/promotion (13% off)

**Budget allocation:**
- Ad 1 (brand refresh): USD 100/week
- Ad 2 (menu): USD 100/week
- Ad 3 (locations): USD 100/week
- Ads 4–5 (social proof + offer): USD 100/week combined

**KPI:** 3,000–4,000 impressions/week, <1.50 THB CPC (cost per click)

**Targeting strategy:**
- Primary: Phuket locals, middle-income +, ages 20–50, interests in food/dining
- Secondary: Tourists in Phuket (lookalike of website visitors + email list)
- Lookalike: Create lookalike from Grab order data (if available)

### Week 2–4: USD 500/month sustained (optimize based on performance)

**Audience:** Refine based on Week 1 performance (which locations, which menu items driving traffic?)

**Creative:** Rotate best-performing ads from Week 1, add customer testimonials, seasonal content

**Budget allocation (example if dine-in performing well):**
- Location ads: 40% (USD 200 — drive footfall by location)
- Menu ads: 30% (USD 150 — drive higher-value orders)
- Offer ads: 20% (USD 100 — drive repeat visits)
- Tests: 10% (USD 50 — test new angles)

**KPI:** Increase to 4,000–5,000 impressions/week, improve ROAS (return on ad spend for dine-in)

## Notes & Constraints

- **5 franchises = complexity** — Consider location-specific messaging, but keep brand consistent
- **Dine-in is the goal** — Emphasis on coming in-store, not just delivery
- **Competitive landscape** — PRIME Burger and Bartels are strong. Our angle: local, community, fun (not premium/trendy)
- **No health claims** — "Fresh" is fine, "made daily" is fine, "nutritious" is risky
- **Fun tone** — This isn't fancy. It's casual, approachable, inviting
- **Flexibility** — If a location is underperforming, we can boost ads for that area mid-campaign
