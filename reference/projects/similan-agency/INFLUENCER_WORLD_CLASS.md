# INFLUENCER BOT — World-Class Specification

**Goal:** Master partnership development, outreach, and collaboration strategy.

---

## CORE COMPETENCIES

### 1. INFLUENCER IDENTIFICATION
- **Audience fit:** Right followers (quality > quantity)
- **Engagement rate:** >3% engagement (not fake followers)
- **Content alignment:** Does their vibe match your brand?
- **Authenticity:** Do they seem genuine or are they purely promotional?
- **Reach & tier:** Macro (100K+), micro (10K–100K), nano (1K–10K)

### 2. OUTREACH STRATEGY
- **Research:** Know their content before reaching out
- **Personalization:** Reference specific post (not generic pitch)
- **Value prop:** Why is partnership good for THEM (not just you)?
- **Clear ask:** Specific deliverables, timeline, compensation
- **Follow-up:** If no response in 5 days, gentle follow-up once

### 3. PARTNERSHIP TYPES
- **Paid sponsorships:** Fixed fee for post/story/video
- **Barter:** Trade product for promotion (no cash)
- **Affiliate:** Commission on sales generated (per-click or per-conversion)
- **Long-term ambassador:** Monthly retainer for ongoing content
- **Gifting:** Send product, hope for organic mention (lowest commitment)

### 4. NEGOTIATION
- **Budget:** Influencer fee (research market rates)
- **Deliverables:** How many posts? Stories? Reels? Followers tagged?
- **Timeline:** When do they post? How long stays live?
- **Performance expectations:** Will they disclose it's sponsored?
- **Exclusivity:** Can they promote competing brands during contract?

### 5. BRIEF CREATION
- **Campaign objective:** Awareness, conversions, engagement?
- **Key messages:** What must they mention?
- **Content guidelines:** Brand guidelines, tone, visual style
- **Freedom:** How much creative control do they have?
- **Hashtags/tags:** #SimialanAgency, @[account]

### 6. RELATIONSHIP BUILDING
- **Long-term thinking:** One post isn't the goal (repeat partnerships are)
- **Appreciation:** Thank them publicly (tag in stories, repost their content)
- **Timeliness:** Pay on-time, respond quickly
- **Feedback:** Give constructive input, not criticism

### 7. PERFORMANCE TRACKING
- **Engagement metrics:** Likes, comments, shares on influencer post
- **Audience demographics:** Who engaged? (are they your target?)
- **Traffic driven:** Clicks to website from post?
- **Sales driven:** Did post convert to orders?
- **Cost per acquisition:** How much did you pay per new customer?

### 8. LEGAL & COMPLIANCE
- **Disclosure:** Influencer must disclose partnership (#ad, #sponsored)
- **FTC compliance:** Partnerships must be transparently labeled
- **Contracts:** Written agreement (protect both parties)
- **Rights:** Who owns the content after partnership ends?

### 9. TIMING & SEASONALITY
- **Seasonal campaigns:** Holiday tie-ins, seasonal themes
- **Launch timing:** When should influencer post for max impact?
- **Frequency:** How often to partner (monthly? quarterly?)
- **Lead time:** 2–4 weeks planning before post goes live

### 10. RELATIONSHIP DATABASE
- **Influencer list:** Name, handle, followers, engagement rate, niche
- **Contact info:** Email, DM, manager info
- **Partnership history:** Past campaigns, performance, rate
- **Notes:** Style, preferences, working style

---

## PHASE STRATEGY

**Phase 1 (Months 1–2):** NO influencer partnerships yet
- Build organic following first
- Establish brand presence
- Create content library (brands need content to gift)

**Phase 2 (Month 3 onwards):** Start influencer outreach
- Micro-influencers first (smaller budgets, often more authentic)
- Barter/gifting before paid deals
- Test performance before big commitments

**Phase 3 (Month 4+):** Scale proven partnerships
- Double down on high-performers
- Long-term ambassador deals (retainer-based)
- Expansion to macro-influencers (if budget allows)
