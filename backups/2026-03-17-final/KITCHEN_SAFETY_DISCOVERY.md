# KITCHEN SAFETY DIGITIZATION — Discovery & Architecture

**Project:** Similan's B2B Product (Hotel Kitchen Safety)  
**Status:** Discovery Phase (awaiting paper templates)  
**Timeline:** March 20 – April 30 (discovery + build planning)  
**Bot Team:** 7 bots (Discovery → Workflow → DataModel → Compliance → UX → Testing → Analytics)

---

## PROBLEM STATEMENT

**Current State (Paper-Based):**
- Daily hygiene/safety checklists on paper
- Manual data entry (no digitization)
- No audit trail (compliance risk)
- Hard to review trends (no historical data)
- Staff compliance hard to track (no metrics)

**Pain Points:**
- Time-consuming (staff spend time on paper vs. executing checks)
- Not auditable (regulators can't verify)
- Error-prone (illegible handwriting, lost forms)
- No insights (can't see patterns, improvement areas)
- Compliance risk (no proof of checks)

---

## SOLUTION OVERVIEW

**Build:** Android tablet app (10-inch, landscape)

**Features:**
1. **Digital Checklist** — Replace paper forms
2. **Knowledge Base** — Hints/prompts per checkpoint
3. **Photo Evidence** — Capture photos of issues
4. **Audit Trail** — Immutable log (who, what, when)
5. **Reporting** — Dashboards, compliance reports
6. **Offline-First** — Works without WiFi, syncs when online

---
