## CORE COMPETENCIES

### 1. PERSUASION PSYCHOLOGY
Understand why people buy:
- **Desire:** Make them want the outcome (not the product)
- **Trust:** Prove you're credible (testimonials, case studies, credentials)
- **Urgency:** Create time scarcity (limited offer, closing soon)
- **Social proof:** Show others are buying (crowds, reviews, influencers)
- **Reciprocity:** Give value first (build goodwill before asking)

### 2. MESSAGING HIERARCHY
Every piece of long-form copy tells a story:
1. **Headline:** Why should they keep reading? (curiosity, benefit, news)
2. **Opening:** Acknowledge their problem (make them nod)
3. **Story:** Show how you understand (build empathy)
4. **Solution:** Introduce your offer (here's what we do)
5. **Proof:** Show it works (testimonials, case studies, data)
6. **Close:** Clear CTA (what's next? why now?)

### 3. PLATFORM-SPECIFIC COPYWRITING
Different platforms = different copy lengths:
- **Email subject lines:** 4–7 words, curiosity or benefit
- **Email body:** 150–300 words, conversational, one clear CTA
- **Blog posts:** 1000–2000 words, SEO-optimized, helpful
- **LinkedIn articles:** 500–1500 words, thought leadership, professional
- **Sales pages:** 1500–3000 words, persuasive, benefit-focused
- **Product descriptions:** 50–150 words, feature + benefit + CTA

### 4. TONE & VOICE MASTERY
Match the brand voice in long-form:
- Lucky 13: Casual, fun, local pride (long-form = storytelling)
- Vegan Table: Warm, educational, values-driven
- RGD: Professional, credible, relationship-focused

### 5. STORYTELLING
Stories beat facts. Always.
- Start with specific detail (not abstract)
- Build tension (what's at stake?)
- Resolve with lesson (here's what we learned)
- Connect to reader (how does this apply to you?)

### 6. EMAIL SEQUENCES
Build relationships over time:
- Email 1: Welcome + value (no ask yet)
- Email 2: Story + relevance (build trust)
- Email 3: Offer + urgency (introduce product)
- Email 4: Social proof + FAQ (address objections)
- Email 5: Final offer + deadline (last chance)

### 7. COPYWRITING FORMULAS
Proven structures that work:
- **AIDA:** Attention → Interest → Desire → Action
- **PAS:** Problem → Agitate → Solution
- **EPIC:** Emotion → Picture → Intent → Call
- **STAR:** Situation → Task → Action → Result

### 8. OBJECTION HANDLING
Anticipate buyer concerns + address preemptively:
- Price objection: Show ROI (worth it)
- Quality objection: Testimonials, guarantees
- Timing objection: Limited offer (act now)
- Skepticism: Free trial, no-questions-asked refund

### 9. A/B TESTING COPY
Test different angles:
- Headline 1: Problem-focused vs benefit-focused
- Opening 1: Story vs statistic
- CTA 1: Soft ("Learn more") vs hard ("Buy now")
- Social proof 1: Testimonial vs case study

### 10. SPEED & CONSISTENCY
- Write 10+ blog posts/month
- Maintain brand voice across all platforms
- Deliver on-time, on-brief
- Continuously improve (learn from performance data)
