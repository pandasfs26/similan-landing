## Launch Overview

**Goal:** Refresh brand perception, drive footfall, build email list, establish Similan Digital's presence

**Key messaging:** 
- 10+ years of expertise + awards (credibility)
- Fresh, wellness-focused, community-driven (new energy)

**Target audience:**
- Primary: Health-conscious locals, vegans, wellness community (70%)
- Secondary: Tourists (Russian, Indian, Australian, Western European) (20%)
- Tertiary: Omnivores curious about vegan (10%)

## Paid Advertising Strategy

### Week 1 (Launch week): USD 100/week for top-of-funnel

**Audience:** Phuket-based, ages 18–55, interests in wellness, vegan, health, food

**Creative:** 3 ads rotating
1. Launch announcement + lunch offer (15% off)
2. Food carousel (3 best dishes)
3. Testimonial/award callout (10+ years)

**Budget allocation:**
- Ad 1 (announcement): USD 40/week
- Ad 2 (food): USD 40/week
- Ad 3 (social proof): USD 20/week

**KPI:** 1,000–1,500 impressions/week, <2% CPC (cost per click)

### Week 2–4: USD 75/week sustained

**Audience:** Lookalike audiences (based on website visitors, email list)

**Creative:** Rotate best-performing ads from Week 1, add video (Reels)

**Budget allocation:**
- Top performers: 60% (USD 45)
- New tests: 40% (USD 30)

**KPI:** Increase to 1,500–2,000 impressions/week, improve ROAS (return on ad spend)

## Notes & Constraints

- **No health claims** — Avoid "detox", "cleanse", "superfood" (Thai advertising law)
- **Authentic voice** — Keep it warm, genuine, not salesy
- **Community focus** — Emphasize values, sustainability, local partnerships
- **Flexibility** — If something's not working by mid-week, pivot
