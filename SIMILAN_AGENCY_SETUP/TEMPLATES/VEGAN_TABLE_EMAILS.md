# The Vegan Table — Email Templates
**Client:** The Vegan Table | **Agency:** Similan Digital
**Version:** 1.0 | **Status:** Draft for Client Review

**Usage Notes:**
- Replace all `[bracketed variables]` with live data before sending
- HTML-ready plain text — copy into your email platform (Mailchimp, Klaviyo, etc.)
- All subject lines are A/B tested — split 50/50 for first 4 hours, then send winner to remainder
- Compliance: All templates include unsubscribe link as required by Thai PDPA and international email law
- Tone: Warm, genuine, community-first. Not promotional. Not corporate.

## TEMPLATE 6: REFERRAL EMAIL

**Goal:** Generate word-of-mouth through warm, non-pushy referral ask. Reward both referrer and new guest.

**Preheader Text:** A simple way to share something you love — and a thank you from us.

---

---

If you enjoyed it — and we hope you did — we'd love to ask you something:

**Do you know someone who would love it too?**

It doesn't have to be a fellow vegan. Some of our most enthusiastic guests are people who'd never considered plant-based dining before they walked through our door. All they needed was a recommendation from someone they trusted.

That could be you.

---

**Here's how it works:**

1. Share your unique referral link with a friend: **[REFERRAL_LINK]**
   *(Or simply forward this email to them — that works too)*

---

**No pressure, no gimmicks.** If you're not comfortable referring us, please don't. We'd rather earn a recommendation genuinely than ask for one that doesn't feel right.

But if The Vegan Table has become a place you love — sharing it is the kindest thing you can do for someone else. And for us.

## EMAIL PLATFORM SETUP NOTES

**Recommended platforms:** Mailchimp (budget-friendly), Klaviyo (better automation), or MailerLite (good for small lists)

**List segmentation to build:**
- All subscribers
- Active (opened in last 30 days)
- Lapsed (no open in 60–90 days)
- Event attendees
- VIP / regulars (manually tagged)

**Automations to activate first:**
1. Welcome Email — trigger: new subscriber
2. Win-Back — trigger: 90 days no engagement
3. Post-event thank you — trigger: [manual send after each event]

**Compliance:**
- Thailand PDPA requires consent at point of collection. Ensure signup forms include explicit opt-in checkbox.
- All emails must include a working unsubscribe link
- Physical address must appear in footer
