## PRODUCTION NOTES FOR CREATIVE TEAM

### Meta Ad Specs
- Feed video: 1:1 (1080×1080) or 4:5 (1080×1350) — mobile-optimised
- Stories/Reels: 9:16 (1080×1920)
- Carousel: 1:1 per frame
- Primary text: 125 characters for best display (125 max before truncation)
- Headline: 40 characters
- File: MP4/MOV (video), JPG/PNG (static) — max 30MB
- Ensure no text covers more than 20% of image area

### TikTok Ad Specs
- Video: 9:16 vertical, 1080×1920
- Duration: 9–60 seconds (15–30 seconds recommended for best CPM)
- Caption: up to 100 characters (keep short — creative does the work)
- Sound: ON by default on TikTok — sound design is critical
- DO NOT: Over-produce. If it looks like a TV ad, reshoot.

### Brand Voice Checklist (All Ads)
- ✅ Fun, approachable, a bit cheeky
- ✅ "Come in" language throughout dine-in ads
- ✅ Real people, real branches — no stock photography
- ✅ Sandwich is always the hero
- ❌ No health claims of any kind
- ❌ No premium/luxury positioning
- ❌ No stiff corporate language
- ❌ No over-produced glossy visuals (especially TikTok)
