## DESIGN & BRAND NOTES (All Templates)

### Colours
- Primary: [Lucky 13 brand colour — e.g., red, orange, yellow — TBC with client]
- Background: White or off-white
- Text: Dark charcoal (not pure black)
- CTA Buttons: Primary brand colour, white text, rounded corners

### Typography
- Headers: Bold, slightly oversized — fun not corporate
- Body: Clean sans-serif, 16px minimum (mobile-readable)
- No ALL CAPS in body copy (only used sparingly for emphasis)

### Tone Checklist
- ✅ Casual and warm
- ✅ Cheeky but not try-hard
- ✅ Direct CTAs — "Come in", not "We invite you to consider visiting"
- ✅ Acknowledge the human behind the email
- ❌ No corporate language ("leverage", "utilise", "please find below")
- ❌ No excessive exclamation marks (1-2 per email max)
- ❌ No health claims

### Images (All Templates)
- Hero sandwich image at top (bright, appetite-driven)
- Branch photos where relevant (real branch, real staff — not stock)
- Keep email width to 600px for desktop/mobile compatibility
