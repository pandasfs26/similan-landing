## TEMPLATE SPECS (7 Types)

### **TEMPLATE 1: INSTAGRAM FEED POST**

- **Dimensions:** 1080 × 1080 px (square)
- **Quantity:** 3 (one per brand, apply matching Brand Kit)
- **Design specs:**
  - Clean, minimal layout
  - One clear focal point (image area)
  - Text area visible but not dominant
  - Logo in subtle corner (top-right or bottom-right, small)
  - Use brand colors as accents (borders, overlays, minimal)
  - White space encouraged (don't overcrowd)
  - No placeholder text (bot will add copy)
- **Quality checklist:**
  - ✅ On-brand colors applied
  - ✅ Typography locked (headline + body fonts)
  - ✅ Logo visible but subtle
  - ✅ Image area clear for bot to upload photo
  - ✅ Readable on mobile
  - ✅ Professional appearance

**Bot use:** Instagram feed post each week (3–5 per brand)

---

### **TEMPLATE 2: INSTAGRAM CAROUSEL (5-SLIDE)**

- **Dimensions:** 1080 × 1080 px per slide (5 slides total)
- **Quantity:** 3 (one per brand)
- **Design specs:**
  - **Slide 1:** Hook — eye-catching visual, minimal text (stop the scroll)
  - **Slide 2:** Progressive story — introduce topic
  - **Slide 3:** Build — detail or context
  - **Slide 4:** Climax — key benefit or insight
  - **Slide 5:** CTA — call-to-action (visit, order, click)
  - All slides branded (colors, fonts consistent)
  - Text areas clear for bot customization
- **Quality checklist:**
  - ✅ Slide 1 is compelling (people will swipe)
  - ✅ Story arc is clear (setup → conflict → resolution)
  - ✅ CTA on final slide is obvious
  - ✅ All slides feel cohesive (same brand)

**Bot use:** Carousel posts 1–2x per week per brand

---

### **TEMPLATE 3: FACEBOOK POST**

- **Dimensions:** 1200 × 628 px (16:9 horizontal)
- **Quantity:** 3 (one per brand)
- **Design specs:**
  - Left side: Image area (60% width)
  - Right side: Text area + CTA button space (40% width)
  - Brand colors used strategically
  - Text overlay max 20% of image (Facebook rules)
  - CTA button space obvious
  - Responsive to mobile view
- **Quality checklist:**
  - ✅ Image area is prominent
  - ✅ Text area is usable
  - ✅ CTA button placement is clear
  - ✅ Looks good at thumbnail size

**Bot use:** Facebook posts 3–4x per week per brand

---

### **TEMPLATE 4: INSTAGRAM REEL THUMBNAIL**

- **Dimensions:** 1080 × 1920 px (9:16 vertical)
- **Quantity:** 3 (one per brand)
- **Design specs:**
  - Full-screen, vertical-first design
  - Text safe area: center 80% (edges may crop on some phones)
  - Hook-focused: first frame must stop scroll (visual priority)
  - Logo in corner (small, doesn't interfere)
  - Bold, high-contrast design
  - Thumbnail still (first frame) is the key — make it pop
  - No small text (unreadable on phone)
- **Quality checklist:**
  - ✅ First frame is compelling (stops scroll)
  - ✅ Text is LARGE and readable at phone size
  - ✅ Safe area respected (center 80%)
  - ✅ On-brand and professional

**Bot use:** Reels 3–5x per week per brand

---

### **TEMPLATE 5: TIKTOK VIDEO TEMPLATE**

- **Dimensions:** 1080 × 1920 px (9:16 vertical)
- **Quantity:** 3 (one per brand)
- **Design specs:**
  - Same dimensions as Reel (vertical)
  - **AUTHENTICITY over polish** — raw beats overproduced
  - Minimal overlays/effects (let content shine)
  - Captions/text: max 4–6 words, BOLD, HIGH CONTRAST
  - Text safe area: center (edges will crop)
  - Hook-focused: first 1–3 seconds critical
  - First frame is thumbnail (make it count)
  - Audio integration: text overlay for trending sound name (optional but recommended)
  - Length: 15–30 seconds optimal
  - Logo: subtle corner placement (don't dominate)
- **Quality checklist:**
  - ✅ First 3 seconds stop scroll (visual hook)
  - ✅ Text is readable at full phone resolution
  - ✅ Captions are concise (4–6 words max)
  - ✅ Design feels authentic (not corporate/polished)
  - ✅ Safe area respected

**TikTok-specific notes:**
- TikTok values authentic, raw content (Gen Z audience)
- Trending audio is **non-negotiable** (15x higher reach)
- Subtitles for sound-off viewing (crucial)
- First frame sets tone — hook hard
- Avoid overproduction; authenticity wins

**Bot use:** TikToks 2–3x per week per brand

---
